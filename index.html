ScoreKeeper+ Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

ScoreKeeper+ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

ScoreKeeper+ may collect the following information:

  • Google Account Information — Only if you choose to sign in using Google. This includes your name and email address.
  • In-App Data — Rooms, teams, scores, and usernames created within the app.
  • Device Information — Anonymous data such as device type and OS version.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Allow you to sign in and manage rooms
  • Sync your data across your device
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or to provide essential services (e.g., Google authentication).

4. Children's Privacy

ScoreKeeper+ does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us so we can remove it.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard methods (such as secure on-device storage) to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request clarification about how your data is used
